This website shows my childhood.

My childhood was quite diverse. Every summer I would visit my grandparents. My sweetest childhood memory was my grandmother's baked khachapuri and compote.I would also like to mention that I had many friends, we would always hang out in my grandmother's garden and steal fruits and vegetables. These memories came back to me when I grew up because I didn't pay attention to them before. I thought that would be the end of it and nothing would take me away from this village, but I was wrong. I haven't been with them for a long time and I always miss them.
